Security: 

Passwords: 
follow general best practices (pretty obvious)

Password managers: 

Kneepass: lets you manage your passwords in a local encrypted database so you only have to worry about remembering one password

Lastpass: Online password management system

1Password: Lastpass competitor that many have been moving to


Public Key Authentication:
generates pair of cryptographic keys (public and private)

SSH: 
makes use of public key auth. to allow for passwordless and non-interactive access to remote hosts. 
to use we need to generate keys and add them to authorized keys list.
